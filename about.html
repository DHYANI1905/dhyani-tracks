Who We Are?

Dhyani Tracks, formerly known as Dhyani Military Evam Sports Store, was conceived in the year 1998 by Hari Prasad Dhyani, who owned a shop that sold goods related to the army. Supplying products from one unit to another is part of our job as merchants, so sometimes we face issues with bad quality, products that are not so good, durability, deadstock, delays in delivering, high costs, etc., and many more issues. At last, we decided to manufacture sportswear products by ourselves and expand it supplier as well as manufacturer, so in 2016, we finally established a sportswear manufacturing plant where we sew cloths and are able to customise products as per the customers' requirements. Till now, we have been developing and trying to provide genuine products at durable rates, while maintaining good quality too.
